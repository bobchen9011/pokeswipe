Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

PokeSwipe ("we", "our", or "the site") is a free, anonymous Pokémon GO friend-code sharing platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect as little data as possible:

  • Uploaded images — Screenshots you voluntarily upload are stored on Cloudinary (a third-party cloud storage service). Images are public and accessible by anyone who visits the site.
  • Anonymous Trainer ID — A random identifier is generated and stored in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device and is not linked to any personal identity.
  • Upload quota — The number of uploads you have made in the past 24 hours is stored in localStorage on your device only.

We do not collect your name, email address, IP address, or any account information. No registration is required.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

This site uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your anonymous Trainer ID and upload count. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Third-party services (Google AdSense, Cloudinary) may set their own cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudinary — Used to store and serve uploaded images. Cloudinary Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Used to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads.
  • Google Fonts — Fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google Privacy Policy

4. User-Generated Content

Images uploaded by users are Pokémon GO screenshots taken by the uploader. By uploading, you confirm you have the right to share the image. Uploaded images are public. Do not upload images containing private personal information beyond your in-game friend code.

We do not review uploaded content in real time. If you find inappropriate content, please contact us.

5. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has uploaded content, please contact us and we will remove it.

6. Data Retention

Uploaded images are retained on Cloudinary indefinitely unless removed. LocalStorage data is retained until you clear your browser data. We do not maintain any server-side user database.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is typically no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about an image you uploaded, contact us and we will remove it from Cloudinary.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us via the GitHub repository: github.com/bobchen9011/pokeswipe
