Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

PokeSwipe ("we", "our", or "the site") is a free, anonymous Pokémon GO friend-code sharing platform operated by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using the site you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect as little data as technically possible:

  • Uploaded images. Screenshots you voluntarily upload are stored on Cloudinary (a third-party image CDN). Images are public and accessible by anyone who visits the site. The 12-digit friend code visible inside the screenshot is the entire purpose of the upload — do not upload images containing any other personal information.
  • Anonymous Trainer ID. A randomly generated identifier stored in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device, is not linked to any personal identity, and resets if you clear browser data.
  • Upload quota. The number of uploads you have made today is stored in localStorage on your device only.
  • Locally cached app state. Lists of which cards you have viewed, which uploads are yours, and your selected language are stored in localStorage only.

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, IP address (beyond what your browser automatically sends to Cloudinary/Netlify for the request itself), geolocation, browser fingerprint, or any account information. No registration is required.

2. Cookies & Local Storage

PokeSwipe itself does not set any cookies. We use the browser's localStorage to store your anonymous Trainer ID, language preference, and upload count. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-party services we rely on (Cloudinary for image hosting, Google Fonts for typography) may set their own cookies or use other tracking technologies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudinary — Used to store and serve uploaded images. Cloudinary Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Used to load the "Bungee" and "Noto Sans TC" fonts. Google may log your IP address as part of normal HTTP request handling. Google Privacy Policy
  • Netlify — Hosts the static website. Netlify may collect basic request metadata (IP, user-agent) for security and abuse prevention. Netlify Privacy Policy

Future advertising: If we ever introduce advertising (e.g. via Google AdSense), this section will be updated to disclose the ad partner and your opt-out options. We will display a cookie consent banner before any advertising cookies are set, where required by law (e.g. GDPR / ePrivacy in the EU).

4. User-Generated Content

Images uploaded by users are Pokémon GO screenshots taken by the uploader themselves. By uploading, you confirm that:

  • You took the screenshot yourself, in your own copy of the game.
  • You have the right to share it publicly.
  • The image does not contain any personal information beyond your in-game friend code.
  • The content does not violate the rights of any third party.

Uploaded images are public. Do not upload images containing private information. We do not perform real-time human review of uploaded content. If you find inappropriate or copyright-infringing content, please report it via our contact page.

5. Children's Privacy

PokeSwipe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has uploaded content that should be removed, please contact us immediately and we will remove it.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Uploaded images are retained on Cloudinary indefinitely unless explicitly removed. LocalStorage data is retained on your device until you clear your browser data. We do not maintain any server-side user database.

To request deletion of an uploaded image, please use the contact page with the image's public_id or the 12-digit friend code. We will action removal within 7 days.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because we do not collect personal data, there is typically no personal data to access, correct, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about any data we may hold about you.
  • Rectify: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erase: Request removal of any uploaded image you submitted.
  • Withdraw consent: Stop using the site at any time. Clear browser data to remove all local state.
  • Lodge a complaint: Contact your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

8. Data Security

All data transmission uses HTTPS. Image storage is delegated to Cloudinary, which provides industry-standard security. We do not run our own backend servers, which means there is no central database that can be breached.

However, please remember: uploaded screenshots are public by design. Anyone who visits PokeSwipe can see them. Treat any image you upload as if it were posted on a public Twitter / X account.

9. International Users

PokeSwipe is hosted on Netlify's global CDN and Cloudinary's global CDN, both of which operate worldwide. By using the site, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These third parties have their own safeguards in place; please refer to their privacy policies linked above.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Significant changes will be announced on the homepage for at least 7 days. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach us via:
